how i think

research first —

decisions should be grounded in evidence, not assumption. i start every project by understanding the people it's for before touching a single frame.

systems thinking —

good design scales. i think in components, patterns and flows — not one-off screens. consistency is a feature, not a constraint.

ship it —

a good idea delivered beats a perfect idea delayed. i work in the open, iterate quickly, and care about what lands in production — not just what looks good in Figma.
